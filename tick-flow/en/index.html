Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application TickFlow, developed by Aners, handles information.

Information Collection

TickFlow does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The application works completely offline and does not require user registration or personal information.

Local Data Storage

The application may store small pieces of data locally on your device, such as application settings (for example selected language or user preferences).

This information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to any external server.

Permissions

The application may use the following permissions:

  • Vibration – used to provide vibration alerts for timer events.
  • Audio – used to play timer sounds.

Notifications

TickFlow may display notifications to show the status of running timers while the application is active or running in the background.

These notifications are used only for timer functionality and do not collect, store, or transmit personal information.

Device Information

The application may read basic device information such as the system language in order to automatically select the appropriate interface language.

This information is processed only on the device and is not transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

TickFlow does not use third-party analytics, advertising services, or external tracking technologies.

Children’s Privacy

TickFlow does not knowingly collect personal information from children or users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
